About Ideathon

Participants will be given many problem statements from various domains, or they can choose their own problem statement, and they must come up with a thorough solution to the problem they have chosen within 16-18 hours. Although implementations are not necessary, a thorough approach to the problem in the form of a paper is anticipated. They can also use graphic components to express their ideas. It is entirely up to them whether they want to implement or construct a prototype.

Tracks

Equitable access to Basic needs: Food, Healthcare, Housing & more

In this track, domain experts who work in Food and nutrition, healthcare, health and wellness, and more will present problem prompts from their work on the ground. Speakers will present real-life case studies that will help you understand how big problems manifest in real life.

Fostering civil discourse: Enabling dialog through education, media and more

In this track, domain experts who work in mission-driven enterprises in education, media, government, and more will present problem prompts from their work on the ground. Speakers will present real-life case studies that will help you understand how big problems manifest in real life.

Open Innovation

This prompt is meant to spark ideation by participants to choose problems they want to solve – participants are free to choose problems that do not fit any of these tracks as well.
